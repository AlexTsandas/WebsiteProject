Fitness

Fitness has always been a passion of mine. I used to practice Judo and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, pushing my limits both physically and mentally. However, now I’ve shifted my focus to weightlifting, where I find immense strength and satisfaction in every lift. Weightlifting has become my way to challenge myself, stay fit, and continually grow stronger.

Music

Music has been a big part of my life for as long as I can remember. I play both the piano and the guitar, and my years of music theory classes have given me a solid foundation to understand and create music. Whether it’s playing a complex piano piece or strumming a familiar melody on the guitar, music is where I focus and express myself best. It’s not just an interest—it’s a skill I’ve worked hard to develop and truly enjoy.
